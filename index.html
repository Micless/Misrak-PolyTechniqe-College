Who We Are

Training built on the workshop floor, not just the whiteboard

Founded to close the gap between classroom theory and factory-floor reality, Misrak Polytechnic College delivers competency-based training aligned with Ethiopia's Occupational Standards. Every program pairs licensed instructors with real tools, real machines, and real assessments.

  • Industry-linked curriculum

    Programs co-designed with manufacturing, construction, and ICT employers.

  • Hands-on workshops

    Fully equipped labs for automotive, electrical, and fabrication trades.

  • Recognized certification

    COC-aligned assessments recognized across Ethiopian industry.
